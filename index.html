About

Creative Director • Designer • Educator

I design and lead creative workshops in primary schools, bringing together illustration, storytelling, animation, and digital media to spark imagination, build confidence, and foster creative thinking in young learners. I have contributed to Creative Cluster projects and work as a TAP facilitator.

I deliver industry-led training through Skillnet’s Print & Design Network, supporting designers and creative professionals in developing practical, career-focused skills across graphic design, digital media, and emerging creative technologies.

Alongside this, I lecture in graphic design, animation, computer programming, and digital media, guiding students to develop strong conceptual thinking and the technical expertise needed for the creative industries.

As a Creative Director, I collaborate with clients on branding, illustration, and motion design, crafting considered visual solutions that communicate clearly, engage audiences, and bring ideas to life.

Creativity is at the heart of everything I do, connecting imagination with practical skills and real-world application.

I believe great design tells stories, builds confidence, and I love helping others bring their creative ideas to life.
